## 🧠 The Idea  

GETHYPED isn’t just a website.  
It’s a fast, bold, and energetic digital experience built for a social-first content agency.

The goal was clear:  
Create something that immediately grabs attention and keeps users engaged through motion and clean structure.

---

## ⚡ Experience Highlights  

- Motion that guides attention, not distracts  
- Clean sections that tell a story  
- Strong visual identity  
- Responsive across all devices  
- Built to feel fast and alive
